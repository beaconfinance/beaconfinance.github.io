Web beacons are tiny graphics with a unique identifier that allow us to track usage patterns, count users who have visited a particular page, etc. Web beacons only collect limited information, including a cookie number, time and date of a page view, and a description of the page on which the web beacon resides. These beacons do not carry any personally identifiable information and are only used to track the effectiveness of Website. We use web beacons in connection with Google Analytics service together with Firebase. The information generated related to our Application is used to create reports about the use of the Website. We use this information to develop, test and improve our services further.

Community Guidelines

We'd love your help in keeping Beacon a fun and safe place to communicate with others. Before you post a comment, consider following a few guidelines so everyone has a good experience.

Warning!

Beacon reserves the right to remove any comment that doesn't comply with our guideline or Terms of Service, and to terminate a Beacon Account for a violation.

Best Practices:

  • Be considerate and respectful of your fellow posters-this is a public forum.
  • Write concisely and use punctuation to help others understand you.

What not to do:

  • Don't post profanity, obscenities, abusive language, or otherwise objectionable content.
  • Comments shouldn't harass, abuse, or threaten anyone's personal safety or property, make false statements, defame, or impersonate someone else.
  • Don't post phone numbers, email or mailing addresses, or credit card information. This helps protect yours and others' personal info.
  • Don't copy and post others' material, trademarks, or intellectual property-it's illegal. This includes cutting and pasting content from other websites (comments should be your own original thoughts).
  • Posting HTML, viruses, or other malicious code is a violation of the Beacon Term of Service.
  • Beacon Financials Ltd is not responsible or liable in any way for comments posted by its users.
